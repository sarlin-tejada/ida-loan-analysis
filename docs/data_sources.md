## IDA Statement of Credits, Grants and Guarantees — Historical Data

- **Source:** World Bank Finances One
- **URL:** https://financesone.worldbank.org/Loans-and-Credits/IDA-Statement-Of-Credits-and-Grants-Historical-Dat/tdwh-3krx
- **Data As Of:** March 31, 2026
- **Last Updated by World Bank:** April 10, 2026
- **Downloaded:** April 27, 2026
- **Rows:** 1,543,992
- **Columns:** 30
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International
- **Place file at:** `data/raw/ida_statement_of_credits_grants_and_guarantees_historical_data_04-27-2026.csv`
- **Notes:** Time series dataset. Monthly historical snapshot from April 2011 onward. Each loan appears multiple times — one row per monthly snapshot. All financial amounts in USD at historical exchange rates. IDs starting with IDAB and IDAG are "Guarantees", IDs starting with IDAD, IDAH and IDAE are "Grants" and the rest are "Credits".
